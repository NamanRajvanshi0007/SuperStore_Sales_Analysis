# SuperStore Sales Analysis & Forecasting

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing SuperStore sales data and forecasting future sales trends using Power BI and Python.

The project combines:
- Interactive Business Intelligence Dashboard
- Sales Performance Analysis
- Profitability Insights
- Time Series Forecasting
- Data-Driven Business Decision Support

The dashboard provides meaningful business insights, while forecasting helps predict future sales patterns for strategic planning.

# Key Business Insights

## 1. Sales Performance Overview
- Total Sales: **1.6M**
- Total Quantity Sold: **22K**
- Total Profit: **175K**

### Insight
The business maintains strong revenue generation with healthy profit margins.

---

## 2. Customer Segment Analysis
- Consumer segment contributes the highest sales
- Corporate segment shows stable performance
- Home Office segment contributes comparatively lower revenue

### Business Insight
Consumer customers are the primary revenue driver and should remain the main business focus.

---

## 3. Category-Wise Sales Analysis
Top categories:
- Office Supplies
- Technology
- Furniture

### Insight
Office Supplies generate the highest sales volume, indicating consistent market demand.

---

## 4. Sub-Category Performance
Top-performing products:
- Phones
- Chairs
- Binders

### Business Insight
These products have strong customer demand and high sales contribution.

---

## 5. Payment Mode Analysis
Most preferred payment methods:
- Cash on Delivery (COD)
- Online Payments
- Card Payments

### Insight
Customers prefer flexible and convenient payment methods.

---

## 6. Shipping Mode Analysis
- Standard Class shipping is used the most
- Same Day delivery has the lowest usage

### Insight
Customers prioritize economical shipping options over faster delivery services.

---

## 7. Monthly Sales & Profit Trends
- Significant sales growth observed during year-end months
- Seasonal spikes visible during festive periods

### Business Insight
Seasonal demand patterns can help optimize inventory and marketing strategies.

---

## 8. Regional Analysis
- Different states contribute differently to revenue and profit
- Some high-sales regions generate lower profitability

### Insight
Profit optimization strategies are required for low-margin regions.

# Forecasting Workflow

## 1. Data Cleaning
- Removed missing values
- Converted date columns into datetime format
- Aggregated daily sales data

---

## 2. Exploratory Data Analysis
- Analyzed trends and seasonality
- Visualized sales fluctuations
- Identified sales patterns

---

## 3. Stationarity Testing
- Performed statistical tests
- Applied differencing techniques

---

## 4. Forecasting Model
- Implemented ARIMA model
- Generated future sales predictions
